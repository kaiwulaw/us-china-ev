About this database Read more →

The US-China Automotive Litigation Database is developed at the University of Denver Sturm College of Law. It is the first open-access resource tracking automobile-related court proceedings comparatively across U.S. federal and state courts and Chinese people's courts. Cases are collected and summarized monthly, with coverage spanning product liability, EV technology disputes, emissions enforcement, intellectual property infringement, and consumer protection claims. If you know of any cases we have missed, please contact us.
